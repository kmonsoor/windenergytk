Summary
=======

* A collection of codes that employs the techniques taught in UMass Amherst's Wind Energy Engineering course.
* The codes are presented in six main groups:

  * Data analysis,
  * Data synthesis,
  * Rotor aerodynamics,
  * Electrical,
  * Dynamics, and
  * Turbine/System Performance.
